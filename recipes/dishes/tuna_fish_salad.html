Tuna Fish Salad

Description

This tuna fish is excellent served on top of a green salad or between two pieces of bread with frest lettuce. Sprinkle a little paparika on top to add a little flavor and color.

Ingredients

  • 1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • ¼ cup chopped celery
  • ¼ cup chopped onion
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • ½ teaspoon lemon juice
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch paprika, or to taste

Steps

  1. Combine tuna with
    • mayonnaise
    • celery
    • onion
    • parsley
    • lemon juice
    • garlic
    • powder
    • salt and pepper in a large bowl
  2. Mix well
  3. Season with paprika
  4. refrigerate until chilled
